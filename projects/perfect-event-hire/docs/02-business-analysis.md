# 02 – Business Analysis

## Purpose

This document analyses the business challenges, opportunities and requirements that led to the redevelopment and optimisation of the Perfect Event Hire website.

The objective was to understand how the website could better support customer engagement, strengthen the company's online presence and contribute to business growth.

---

# Business Context

Perfect Event Hire operates in Australia's event hire industry, providing equipment and styling solutions for weddings, corporate events and private celebrations.

The website is one of the company's primary digital assets and plays an important role in attracting customers, presenting services and generating enquiries.

A modern, well-structured website supports marketing efforts while building customer confidence.

---

# Problem Statement

An analysis of the existing website identified several opportunities for improvement.

These included:

- Website navigation could be simplified.
- Important services were difficult to locate.
- SEO opportunities were not fully utilised.
- Some pages lacked consistent structure.
- Mobile responsiveness could be improved.
- Calls-to-action could be made more visible.
- Website content required better organisation.

These issues affected customer experience and limited the effectiveness of the website as a digital marketing platform.

---

# Stakeholders

## Business Owner

Objectives:

- Increase enquiries
- Improve online visibility
- Strengthen the company's digital presence
- Present a professional brand

---

## Customers

Needs:

- Easily browse available services
- Find information quickly
- Request quotations without difficulty
- Navigate the website on both desktop and mobile devices

---

## Search Engines

Requirements:

- Optimised content
- Clear website structure
- Fast loading pages
- Proper metadata
- Strong internal linking

---

# Opportunities Identified

## User Experience

- Improve navigation
- Simplify page layouts
- Improve readability
- Reduce unnecessary clicks

---

## Search Engine Optimisation

- Improve page titles
- Optimise meta descriptions
- Add image ALT text
- Improve keyword targeting
- Strengthen local SEO

---

## Content

- Rewrite service descriptions
- Improve page consistency
- Organise information more clearly
- Create stronger calls-to-action

---

## Branding

- Improve visual consistency
- Present a more professional image
- Increase customer trust

---

# Risks

Without improvement the business could experience:

- Lower search visibility
- Reduced customer engagement
- Fewer enquiries
- Stronger competition from businesses with better digital presence

---

# Recommended Solution

Develop a customer-focused website that combines:

- Modern design
- Responsive layout
- SEO best practices
- Clear navigation
- Well-structured content
- Strong calls-to-action

The solution should support both customer experience and long-term business growth.

---

# Expected Business Benefits

- Improved customer experience
- Increased online visibility
- Stronger brand credibility
- Better SEO performance
- Increased customer enquiries
- Improved digital marketing capability
