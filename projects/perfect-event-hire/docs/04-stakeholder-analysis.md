# 04 – Stakeholder Analysis

## Purpose

This document identifies the key stakeholders involved in the Perfect Event Hire website project and outlines their objectives, expectations and influence on the project.

---

# Primary Stakeholders

## Business Owner

### Objectives

- Increase customer enquiries.
- Improve online visibility.
- Strengthen brand credibility.
- Generate more business opportunities.

### Needs

- Professional website.
- Easy content management.
- Better search engine rankings.
- Positive customer experience.

---

## Customers

### Objectives

- Find services quickly.
- View previous work.
- Compare event hire options.
- Contact the business easily.

### Pain Points

- Difficult navigation.
- Slow loading pages.
- Limited service information.

---

## Search Engines

### Objectives

- Understand website content.
- Crawl pages efficiently.
- Index important pages.

### Requirements

- Structured headings.
- Metadata.
- Internal links.
- Optimised images.

---

## Future Marketing Team

### Objectives

- Publish new content.
- Improve SEO.
- Monitor performance.
- Launch campaigns.

---

# Success Factors

The project succeeds when:

- Business goals are achieved.
- Customers have a positive experience.
