# 01 – Project Overview

## Project Name

Perfect Event Hire Website Development & Digital Optimisation

---

## Executive Summary

Perfect Event Hire is an Australian event hire company specialising in weddings, corporate events and private celebrations.

This project focused on improving the company's online presence by redesigning and optimising its website to create a better customer experience, strengthen search engine visibility and support long-term business growth.

The project combined business analysis, website development, search engine optimisation (SEO) and digital strategy to deliver a practical business solution.

---

## Business Background

The website acts as one of the company's primary marketing channels.

Potential customers rely on the website to:

- Discover available services
- View event hire products
- Explore previous work
- Request quotations
- Contact the business

Improving the website helps strengthen the company's digital presence and supports customer acquisition.

---

## Project Objectives

The project aimed to:

- Improve website usability
- Strengthen SEO
- Improve mobile responsiveness
- Present services professionally
- Increase customer enquiries
- Build a scalable digital platform
