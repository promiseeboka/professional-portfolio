# 06 – Current State Analysis

## Purpose

This document evaluates the existing website before redevelopment.

---

# Current Website Assessment

The existing website successfully introduced the business but presented opportunities for improvement in usability, SEO and customer engagement.

---

# Strengths

- Established online presence.
- Existing customer gallery.
- Basic service information.
- Contact details available.

---

# Weaknesses

- Navigation could be simplified.
- Content structure inconsistent.
- Limited local SEO.
- Images required optimisation.
- Calls-to-action lacked prominence.
- Mobile experience could be improved.

---

# Opportunities

- Improve search rankings.
- Increase enquiries.
- Improve customer confidence.
- Strengthen branding.
- Better content organisation.

---

# Threats

- Strong competition.
- Changing Google algorithms.
- Increasing customer expectations.

---

# Summary

The website provided a solid foundation but required improvements to better support business growth and digital marketing objectives.
